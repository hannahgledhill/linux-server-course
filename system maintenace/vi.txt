i - insert
Esc - escape out of any mode
r - replace
d - delete
:q! - quit without saving
:wq! - quit and save
vi <filename> - either opens or creates the filename
shift zz - saves and exits the filename
dd - delets the current line
u - undoes
/<term> - searches for and places the cursor at the search term
sed - replacing a string in afile with a newstring
can find and delete a line by using sed to replace it with nothing
